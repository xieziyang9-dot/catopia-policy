Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Grand Peak respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your data associated with Cat Girl and our other games on the Google Play platform.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your personal data by following any of the methods below:

Method 1: Email Request

Send an email to xieziyang9@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Please include:

  • Your Google Play account name or email
  • The name of the game(s) you played
  • A brief description of your request

Method 2: Uninstall the App

  1. Uninstall Cat Girl from your device.
  2. Send us an email at xieziyang9@gmail.com to confirm you want all server-side data deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will remove:

  • Your game profile and account information
  • Game progress, scores, and achievements
  • In-game preferences and settings
  • Device identifiers associated with your account
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Processing Time

We will process your data deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been successfully deleted.

Important Notes

  • Data deletion is permanent and irreversible. All game progress and purchases will be lost.
  • We may retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).
  • Deleting your data will not affect any data held directly by Google.

Contact Us
